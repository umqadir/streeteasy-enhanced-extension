FAQ

What is the “Ambient risk index”?
Incidents per 100k average people present (a blend of residents and daytime workers), so business districts aren’t unfairly distorted by low residential population.
What is “Property Crime”?
A simple index of NYPD offense categories: burglary, grand larceny, and grand larceny of motor vehicle.
Why might a neighborhood show 0 murders?
Stats are assigned to the official NYC NTA that contains the listing. NTAs don’t perfectly match StreetEasy neighborhood labels, and incidents near borders can fall into adjacent NTAs.
